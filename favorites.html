🌤️ Favorites – Your Personalized Weather Dashboard

Welcome to your Favorites page — your personal weather hub. This space is designed just for you, to help you stay informed about the weather conditions in the cities and places you care about most.

No more searching again and again — whether it’s your hometown, the place you’re planning to travel, or the city where your loved ones live, you can save them here for quick access.
