About WeatherNow

WeatherNow is a simple and fast tool to check weather updates from around the globe.

It pulls live data and shows accurate forecasts for any city you type.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the wttr.in weather API.

How to Use WeatherNow Effectively

  • Search your city: Simply type the city name and press “Get Weather.”
  • Review current conditions: Temperature, humidity, wind speed, and weather status appear instantly.
  • Check the forecast: See what the next three days hold for your area or travel destination.
  • Save favorites: Bookmark cities you frequently check for quicker access next time.

WeatherNow’s easy-to-navigate layout means you spend less time searching and more time planning your day with confidence.
